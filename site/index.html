How To Install IOS / MAC

Note: Mac Instructions are at the bottom.


IOS

1. Click the download button. Then click the allow button.

2. Click the Install button.

3. Enter your phone passcode.

4. Click the Install button again.

5. Click the Done button.

6. Go back to Settings.

7. Click the Back button.

8. Click the Settings button.

9. Click The VPN menu.

10. Chose SchoolVPN. Then click Status slider.

11. If you See the VPN logo in the top left you are connected. Enjoy!

Mac

1. Click the download button.

2. Click file that was downloaded.

3. Click Continue.

4. There is no Username/Password, continue by pressing the Install button.

5. Enter your mac password. Then click OK.

6. Click the nine squares at the top left.

7. Click Network.

8. Chose SchoolVPN. Then click the connect button.

9. Click the disconnect button to disconnect from SchoolVPN. ENJOY!

How To Install Android

1. Click the download button. Then click the OPEN button.

2. Click the Settings button.

3. Turn On "Allow from this source". Then click the back button.

4. Click the Install button.

5. Click the Install Anyways button.

6. Wait for it to finish Installing. Then click Open the button.

7. Click the connect button.

8. Click OK.

9. If you see the cloud or lock in your notifications you are connected! Enjoy!

🔒

How To Install Chrome

1. Click the Chrome download button.

2. Click "Add to Chrome".

3. Click "Add Extension".

4. Click on the SchoolVPN Logo.

5. Click the Connect Button.

6. If you see the "Connected" message you are done. ENJOY!
